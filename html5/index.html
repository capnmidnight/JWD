Storage

Select the system you would like to use to store your writing data. The storage configuration setting must be set separately for all browsers and computers. All other configuration settings are stored in the selected storage system.

Store the data in your current browser (). You will not be able to share data between browsers or computers. Data will always be immediately available for use.

Store the data in your Dropbox account. You will be able to share data between browsers and computers. Data may not be available without a network connection.

(Eventually) Store the data in your Microsoft OneDrive account. You will be able to share data between browsers and computers. Data may not be available without a network connection.

(Eventually) Store the data in your Google Drive account. You will be able to share data between browsers and computers. Data may not be available without a network connection.

Import from/export to file

Store the data on your computer's file system. You will be able to share data between browsers, but not between computers. Data will always be immediately available for use.

Just Write, Dammit!

This is "Just Write, Dammit!" As in, that's all you can do when you use it. As in, put Twitter/Facebook/G+/Farmville/etc. down and just write, dammit. It blocks out all of the background noise of your computer so you can focus on one thing: writing.

It runs in the browser so it goes where you go. It runs in complete full screen mode (hit F11 on your keyboard). The text is large to encourage flow. The colors are cool to avoid eye strain. A counter tells you how many words you've written so far, and a small timer tells you how long you have been writing. It saves to plain text files and offers very few formatting options. Because you shouldn't be spending time on making words emboldened or italicized or picking a font. You should just be writing, dammit.

So what the hell are you doing? Get to work!
