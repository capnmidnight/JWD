About Just Write, Dammit!

This is "Just Write, Dammit!". JWD is the excuse eliminator for your writing aspirations. It is a distraction-free writing program that syncs your writing across devices, provides easy-to-use analysis features to make you a better writer, and simple document publishing methods to get your writing out to readers. So put Twitter/Facebook/G+/Farmville/etc. down and just write, dammit!

JWD helps serious writers finish their manuscript, fix common style errors, and publish their writing for consumption:

  • Distraction-free writing: run in full-screen mode to block out the noise of the modern, connected life.
  • EPUB package generator1: prepare your writing for eReader devices in a single click.
  • Google Drive integration2: auto-save your writing to your Google Drive, or import writing you already have in Google Drive as a chapter in your manuscript.
  • Dropbox auto-save3: auto-save your writing to your Dropbox.
  • Auto-updating word count and writing timer: know when you've hit your writing quota for the day.
  • Word- and phrase-frequency analysis: find out where you habitually overuse idioms and expressions.
  • Chapter-based organization structure: keep organized and focused on one subject at a time.
  • Minimalist, responsive, cross-platform design4: runs just as well on your smartphone or tablet as it does on your laptop or desktop computer.
  • Backup manuscript to file system: Archive and keep track of multiple book projects at one time.
  1. Amazon Kindle requires a conversion process to be able to display ePub documents. The process is handled automatically by Amazon.
  2. Google Drive integration requires Google login. Use of this feature requires granting the JWD application access to your Google Drive documents. JWD runs completely in your browser and does not relay information about writing to any other party. Currently, no user-specific information is stored at any site accessible to the owner of JWD.
  3. Dropbox integration requires Dropbox login. Use of this features requires granting the JWD application access to a segregated portion of your Dropbox storage. As such, it does not enable access to any files other than those that JWD writes for itself.
  4. Runs on any operating system that provides an HTML5-compliant Web browser, which includes all recent versions of Windows, OSX, Linux, iOS, Android, and *BSD. Currently supporting Google Chrome, Internet Explorer 10+, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Opera. Google Chrome is recommended for the best experience on all operating systems.
